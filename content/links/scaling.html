## Scaling - [Scaling Pinterest - From 0 To 10s Of Billions Of Page Views A Month In Two Years](http://highscalability.com/blog/2013/4/15/scaling-pinterest-from-0-to-10s-of-billions-of-page-views-a.html?utm_source=feedly) Describes how Pinterest started small, scaled up by using a ton of different technologies, but then went with a less complicated but more mature stack, so that they could simply add boxes where needed. Describes EC2, MySQL, Redis, Memcache, Solr. Long analysis and discussion of sharding vs clustering, and pros/cons of each.

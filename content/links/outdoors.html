## Outdoors - [CHAOS: Cal Hiking And Outdoor Society](http://calhikes.dreamhosters.com/) This is an active Berkeley group, dedicated to all outdoor activities. There is a wiki with [cool places](http://calhikes.dreamhosters.com/wiki/cool-places), [how-tos](http://calhikes.dreamhosters.com/wiki/how-tos), [hiking & backpacking links](http://calhikes.dreamhosters.com/wiki/hiking-backpacking), and ([Strawberry Canyon parking](http://calhikes.dreamhosters.com/wiki/strawberry-canyon-parking)) among others.
